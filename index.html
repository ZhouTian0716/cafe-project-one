about us

our head chef han and his team brings in passion and love of food to bare witness. with extensive experience across hatted fine dining restaurants and prestigious hotels, Han extends his skills to his team and brings ideas and dishes to the everyday cafe-goers of rhodes. taking freshest seasonal produce, capturing the best flavour and translating in Bare Witness' unique style is the ethos of his dishes.

we're excited to treat you to our deliciously curated dishes, highlighting the best of fresh seasonal produce. all you need to do is sit back, enjoy the beautiful waterview and indulge in the food.
